7 Best Restaurants with Vegan/Vegetarian Options

Main Squeeze

A cafe in downtown Columbia with vegetarian and vegan breakfast, lunch, and dessert options.

Address: 23 S 9th St, Columbia, MO 65201

Visit Main Squeeze's Website

Cafe Berlin

This restaurant serves vegetarian and vegan breakfast, lunch, and dinner. It's the perfect place for brunch! On weekends, they open up their bar and host live music and entertainment.

Address: 220 N 10th St, Columbia, MO 65201

Visit Cafe Berlin's Website

Seol Taco

A restaurant that specializes in Korean-Mexican fusion. This restaurant has many lunch and dinner options for vegetarians and vegans.

Address: 1020 E Broadway, Columbia, MO 65201

Visit Seoul Taco's Website

Gina's Vegan A Go Go Food Truck

This is not a restaurant, but this vegan food truck specializes in breakfast and lunch options. During the summer, it is parked at the Columbia Farmers Market every Saturday.

Address: Farmers Market 1769 W Ash St, Columbia, MO 65201

Visit Gina's Vegan A Go Go Website

Ozark Mountain Biscuit and Bar

This restaurant is breakfast based and has many vegetarian options. They also have a food truck located at the farmers market.

Address: 1204 Hinkson Ave, Columbia, MO 65201

Visit Ozark Mountain Biscuit and Bar's Website

Kinkao

This is a Thai restaurant that doesn't specialize in vegan or vegetarian dishes, but they do have some vegetarian options.

Address: 904 E Broadway suite 101, Columbia, MO 65201

Visit Kinkao's Website

Beet Box

This is a Middle Eastern restaurant that offers many vegan options. They pride themselves on trying new things every week.

Address: 602 Fay St, Columbia MO 65201

Visit Beet Box's Website
